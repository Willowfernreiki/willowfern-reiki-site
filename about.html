What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice developed in Japan by Dr Mikao Usui.

It supports relaxation, emotional balance, and nervous system regulation. It is not a substitute for medical care.

Sessions are always consent-led and adapted to your comfort.

About the Practitioner

I am trained in both Eastern and Western Reiki lineages with professional practice training in London.

My approach is grounded, calm, and supportive of nervous system regulation.

Each session is held in a safe, non-judgemental space.
