What to Expect

Optional grounding elements such as palo santo, sage, or agua de florida may be used — always with your consent.

Reiki is then offered in a calm, holistic way while you remain fully comfortable and at ease.

Each session is supported by soft, meditative music to encourage deep relaxation and balance.
