About Reiki

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice that originated in Japan in the early 20th century. It was developed by Dr Mikao Usui and is now practiced worldwide in both Eastern and Western traditions.

Reiki works by supporting the body’s natural ability to restore balance and promote relaxation. It is widely used as a complementary practice alongside conventional approaches to wellbeing.

I am trained in both Eastern and Western Reiki lineages, allowing me to offer a grounded and intuitive approach to energy healing. My training was completed at the Reiki Science Academy in London, where the focus is on professional, structured, and responsible practice.

Reiki is a non-manipulative complementary therapy. It is used alongside other treatments and does not replace medical care. Sessions do not involve physical manipulation and are always guided by consent and comfort.

Sessions may be offered hands-on or hands-off depending on your preference. Both methods are equally effective and are always adapted to individual comfort and boundaries.

Reiki is commonly used to support relaxation and emotional wellbeing and is often chosen by individuals experiencing stress, anxiety, burnout, or emotional overwhelm. It is also used in supportive care environments to encourage calm and comfort.
