About Reiki

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive healing practice that works with the body’s natural energy systems. It helps release tension, restore emotional balance, and create space for deep relaxation.

During a session, energy is channelled through the hands to support the flow of energy within the body. This process can promote calm, clarity, and a sense of inner stability.

Each session is intuitive and grounded, allowing your body to receive exactly what it needs in a safe and peaceful environment.
