What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice that originated in Japan in the early 20th century. It was developed by Dr Mikao Usui and is now practiced worldwide in both Eastern and Western traditions.

Reiki works by supporting the body’s natural ability to restore balance and promote relaxation. It is widely used alongside conventional approaches to wellbeing. It does not replace medical care and is always non-manipulative.

Sessions may be offered hands-on or hands-off depending on preference. Both are equally effective and always guided by comfort and consent. Reiki is often used to support stress, anxiety, burnout, and emotional overwhelm.
