What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice that originated in Japan in the early 20th century. It was developed by Dr Mikao Usui and is now practiced worldwide in both Eastern and Western traditions.

Reiki supports the body’s natural ability to restore balance and promote relaxation. It is used alongside conventional approaches to wellbeing; Reiki does not replace medical care.

Sessions are always guided by consent and comfort, and may be offered hands-on or hands-off depending on your preference.
