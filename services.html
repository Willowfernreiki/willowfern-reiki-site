Services

Each session is designed to support your wellbeing through calm, focused energy work tailored to your individual needs. Whether you are seeking relaxation, emotional balance, or a deeper connection to yourself, sessions are offered in a way that feels grounded, supportive, and intuitive.

1:1 Reiki Healing Session

A personalised in-person session offering deep relaxation and energy balancing. This allows space for the body and mind to settle, release tension, and restore natural flow.

Distance Reiki Healing

Receive Reiki energy remotely from the comfort of your own space. Distance sessions are just as effective and provide a gentle, calming experience wherever you are.

Energy Balancing & Emotional Support

A supportive session focused on easing stress, overwhelm, and emotional tension. This work helps create space for clarity, grounding, and a sense of inner calm.

Corporate Reiki

Reiki sessions offered within a workplace setting to support staff wellbeing, reduce stress, and promote a calmer, more balanced working environment. Sessions can be adapted to suit team needs and schedules.

Open-Air Reiki

A unique Reiki experience held outdoors, allowing for a deeper connection with nature. Sessions take place in a mutually agreed setting such as a quiet park or a private garden.

These sessions are weather and temperature dependent and are designed to create a calm, grounded atmosphere where the natural environment supports the healing process.

All sessions are approached with care, respect, and sensitivity, creating a safe and peaceful space for your experience.
