What to Expect

A typical Reiki session takes place in a calm, quiet space where you remain fully clothed and comfortable, either lying down or seated.

The practitioner gently places their hands either lightly on or just above the body, allowing energy to flow where it is needed most.

You may feel deeply relaxed, drift into sleep, or notice warmth, tingling, or gentle energy shifts.

Each experience is individual and unfolds naturally. You remain in full control throughout the session.
