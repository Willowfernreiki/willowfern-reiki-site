Reiki Services

Sessions are designed to support relaxation, emotional balance, and overall wellbeing. Each treatment is tailored to your individual needs and offered in a calm, supportive environment.

1:1 Reiki Healing Session

A private in-person Reiki session focused on deep relaxation and energetic balance. This session supports stress relief, emotional release, and overall wellbeing.

Distance Reiki Healing

Reiki energy is sent remotely, allowing you to receive a calming and restorative experience from your own space. Many clients find this deeply relaxing and effective.

Energy Balancing & Emotional Support

A supportive session designed to help with stress, anxiety, overwhelm, and emotional fatigue. The focus is on restoring calm and grounding.

Corporate Reiki

Workplace Reiki sessions designed to support staff wellbeing, reduce stress levels, and improve overall workplace balance. Sessions can be tailored for individuals or groups.

Open-Air Reiki

A nature-based Reiki experience held outdoors in a park or private garden setting. This session is weather dependent and focuses on grounding, calm, and connection with nature.
