What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice that originated in Japan in the early 20th century. It was developed by Dr Mikao Usui and is now practiced worldwide in both Eastern and Western traditions.

Reiki works by supporting the body’s natural ability to restore balance and promote relaxation. It is widely used alongside conventional approaches to wellbeing. It is a non-manipulative therapy and does not replace medical care. Sessions do not involve physical manipulation and are always guided by consent and comfort.

Sessions may be offered hands-on or hands-off depending on your preference. Both methods are equally effective and are always adapted to individual comfort and boundaries. Reiki is commonly used to support relaxation and emotional wellbeing, and is often chosen by individuals experiencing stress, anxiety, burnout, or emotional overwhelm.

What to Expect

You are welcome to arrive 5–10 minutes before your session to allow time to settle in. We will begin with a brief check-in to understand how you are feeling and to set a gentle intention for the session.

To create a calm and grounded atmosphere, the session may begin with subtle energetic cleansing using palo santo, sage, or agua de florida. These are always optional — please feel free to let me know if you would prefer not to include them.

The space is then gently prepared using sound, often with a kenari shaker, to support relaxation and presence. During the session, I will connect to the Reiki energy and allow it to flow to where it is most needed, while you remain fully clothed and resting comfortably.

Each session is accompanied by a carefully curated playlist of soft, meditative music designed to promote a sense of calm, balance, and ease.
