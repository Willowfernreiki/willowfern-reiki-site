Services

Each session is designed to support your wellbeing through calm, focused energy work tailored to your individual needs. Whether you are seeking relaxation, emotional balance, or deeper grounding, sessions are held in a safe and supportive space.

1:1 Reiki Healing Session

A personalised in-person session designed to support deep relaxation and energetic balance. Sessions allow time for your system to unwind, release tension, and restore natural flow.

Distance Reiki Healing

Reiki energy is sent remotely, allowing you to receive a calming and restorative experience from the comfort of your own space. Many clients find this surprisingly powerful and grounding.

Energy Balancing & Emotional Support

A gentle session focused on stress, emotional overwhelm, and restoring inner calm. This is especially supportive during periods of change or heightened pressure.

Corporate Reiki

Reiki sessions delivered in workplace environments to support staff wellbeing, reduce stress, and encourage a calmer, more balanced working culture.

Open-Air Reiki

A nature-based Reiki experience held outdoors in a mutually agreed space such as a park or garden. These sessions are weather dependent and designed to enhance grounding and connection with nature.
